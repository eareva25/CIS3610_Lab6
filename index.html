UFO - Ford Files

Background

News coverage of UFO in 1966 were explained as “college student pranks, balloons, stars, or air crafts.” This explanation of the UFO phenomenon covered by the media did not satisfy House Republican Leader Gerald, R. Ford. Mr.Ford having political power was able to file a petition to get the right investigators into the matter and give the American people a congruent answer to the UFO sightings.

Execution

What Mr.Ford proposed was to have a hearing of key witnesses like personnel part of the executive branch and eye witnesses of the public to testify about the sightings of UFO. His goal was to bring awareness of the issue and shed light into the matter because the American people deserved an explanation.

Project Blue Book

What is project blue book? The air force created a program that investigated and handled UFO reports. The air force hired over 100 “high caliber” non-biased scientist to investigate the UFOs reports. Many universities were involved in this project. The main campus involved was the University of Colorado.

Results

  • 12,618 UFO sightings were reported and investigated and most were solved.
  • 701 sightings remained unidentified
